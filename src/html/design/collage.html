Mixed Media Collage Series


About

My final project for Intro to Digital Art with Professor Nancy Macko at Scripps College in 2017. I chose to produce six collages using scanned drawings, watercolor paintings and photos from my travels.

Theme

Inspired by my study abroad experience in Jordan where I learned a great deal about the climate crisis facing the Middle East. I wanted to incorporate my nature photography highlighting the beauty of our environment with ink drawings that expressed my anxiety around school and global climate change.
